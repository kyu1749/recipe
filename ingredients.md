# Ingredients
## 5 Spice Rotisserie Chicken
* 1x 3-4lb whole chicken
* 2 tsp onion powder
* 3 tbsp minced garlic
* 1 cup soy sauce
* 1/3 cup sesame oil
* 1 tbsp five spice powder

* 1 onion
* 1 bell pepper

* 3 roma tomatoes
* Bay leaf

* 1/4 cup fresh chopped parsley
* 2 cups Spanish rice
* 5 cups chicken broth

* 8 oz calamari rings
